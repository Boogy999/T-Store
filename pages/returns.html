Returns & Exchange Policy

Easy Returns Process

1

Initiate Return

Log into your account and select the items you wish to return from your order history.

2

Print Label

Download and print your prepaid return shipping label.

3

Pack Items

Pack the items securely in their original packaging if possible.

4

Ship

Drop off your package at any authorized shipping location.

Return Window

Items must be returned within 30 days of delivery. Holiday purchases may have extended return windows.

Item Condition

Items must be unworn, unwashed, and have original tags attached. Returns must include all original packaging.

Exchanges

Need a different size or color? Exchanges are free and easy. Simply select "Exchange" when initiating your return.

Refund Process

Refunds will be issued to your original payment method within 5-7 business days of receiving your return.

Important Notes

  • Sale items are final sale and cannot be returned unless defective
  • Customized items cannot be returned unless defective
  • Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the item is defective
  • International returns may have different policies

Need Help?

Our customer service team is here to assist you with any questions about returns or exchanges.
